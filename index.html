Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. TXT to PDF runs entirely in your browser. No files or text are ever uploaded to any server. Your data remains strictly on your local device.

Does it support images?

Yes, standard markdown images are supported. Ensure the image URLs are publicly accessible, or use Base64 encoded images directly in your document.

What encodings are supported?

We automatically detect a wide variety of encodings including UTF-8, Windows-1252, Shift-JIS, and many more, ensuring your text looks perfect regardless of its origin.

Are the PDFs print-ready?

Yes! You can configure margins, fonts, and A4/Letter paper sizes via our settings menu. The exported documents are fully vectorized and ready for professional printing.

What is the maximum file size?

You can upload text and Markdown files up to 10MB in size. This limit ensures lightning-fast performance and prevents your browser from crashing during the conversion process.

Do I need to install any software?

No installation is required! TXT to PDF is a web-based tool that works directly in modern web browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge without needing any third-party plugins.
