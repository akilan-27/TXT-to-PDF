Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TXT to PDF. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy governs your use of our website and our fast, client-side document conversion tool.

2. Local Processing & Zero Uploads

Our tool is built on a privacy-first, client-side architecture. When you use our converter:

  • Your text files are never uploaded to any external server.
  • All conversions from TXT or Markdown to PDF happen entirely inside your device's web browser using JavaScript.
  • We have absolutely zero access to your document contents.

3. Analytics and Cookies

To improve user experience and support the maintenance of this free tool, we use cookies. We partner with Google AdSense to display advertisements. Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

4. Local Storage

Our application uses your browser's Local Storage to temporarily save your document content and preferences (such as your chosen theme or paper size) between sessions. This data is strictly local and remains on your device until you clear your browser data or use the 'Clear' button within the editor.

5. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
